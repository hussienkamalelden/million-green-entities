FAQ

What exactly is One Million Green Entities?

One Million Green Entities is a digital platform that showcases environmentally and socially conscious organizations, companies, and individuals worldwide. We offer a grid of one million pixels divided into 10,000 cells (each cell containing 100 pixels) for just €25 per cell. When you purchase a cell, your logo and link become part of our global directory of green entities, creating visibility for your organization while funding meaningful social projects.

Who can join the platform?

Any organization, company, or individual that meets our green entity criteria can join. We welcome:

  • Environmental organizations and NGOs
  • Sustainable businesses and social enterprises
  • Companies with strong environmental and social responsibility practices
  • Individuals promoting green initiatives

However, entities must not be involved in any war or conflict-related activities and must demonstrate clear positive impact on society or the environment.

How much does it cost and what do I get?

Each cell costs just €25 and includes:

  • A space of 10,000 cells (each cell containing 100 pixels) on our global grid
  • Your logo displayed prominently
  • A clickable link to your website or social media
  • Permanent placement in our directory of green entities
  • Contribution to funding social impact projects
  • Being part of a global community of environmentally conscious organizations

How do you verify that entities are truly "green"?

We have a thorough verification process that includes:

  • Reviewing your organization's mission and vision statements
  • Examining your actual practices and documented activities
  • Checking your public communications and partnerships
  • Ensuring you meet our core criteria: peace commitment, positive social impact, and mission alignment with sustainability
  • Maintaining quality standards to preserve platform integrity

This process ensures every entity on our platform represents genuine commitment to positive change.

What social projects do you support with the proceeds?

Our first social project focuses on supporting immigrant communities through a catering and kitchen initiative. This project will:

  • Provide culinary training and employment opportunities for immigrants
  • Create sustainable food services for the broader community
  • Build bridges between immigrant and local communities through food and culture
  • Serve as a model for future social projects

The project launches once we reach our sales target, ensuring sufficient funding for meaningful impact.

How long does the joining process take?

The process typically takes 5-10 business days:

  • Step 1: Contact us
  • Step 2: Choose your cells by giving us the cells no.
  • Step 3: Entity verification (2-5 business days)
  • Step 4: Payment details sent via email
  • Step 5: Payment processing and cell reservation
  • Step 6: Go live on the platform (immediate after payment)

We'll keep you informed throughout the entire process via email.

Is my placement on the grid permanent?

Yes! Once you purchase a cell and complete the process, your placement on our grid is permanent. Your logo and link will remain visible to visitors as long as the One Million Green Entities platform exists. This makes your €25 investment a one-time cost for ongoing visibility and participation in our green community.

Additionally, as our platform grows and gains more visitors, the value of your placement increases, giving you long-term benefits from your participation in our initiative.
