What the Initiative Is About

One Million Green Entities is a unique digital platform that brings together environmentally and socially conscious organizations, companies and individuals from around the world. Our concept is simple yet powerful: we offer a grid of one million pixels, divided into cells of 10,000 blocks, each block containing 100 pixels, available for purchase at just €2 per cell.

When green entities purchase a cell, their logo and link become part of our global showcase, creating a visual directory of entities making a positive impact. Each cell serves as a digital window to these entities, allowing visitors to discover and connect with businesses, NGOs, and initiatives that share a commitment to environmental sustainability and social responsibility.

This isn't just advertising – it's community building. Every purchase contributes to funding meaningful social projects, making each participant part of a larger movement for positive change.

The Story of the Initiative

The idea for One Million Green Entities was born from a simple observation: there was no centralized platform collecting and showcasing all the amazing green entities making a difference in our world. As the founder, I noticed this gap and decided to act.

Starting with zero funding and driven purely by passion for environmental and social causes, I began developing this initiative. The concept was inspired by the need to create an affordable, accessible way for green entities to gain visibility while simultaneously funding social projects that matter.

What started as a one-person mission has evolved into a platform designed to connect like-minded organizations and channel their collective power toward meaningful social impact. Every entity that joins our grid becomes part of a story much larger than themselves, a story of global cooperation for a better world.

Our Mission

To create the world's most comprehensive digital showcase of green entities while generating sustainable funding for social impact projects. We exist to:

  • Connect environmentally and socially conscious entities in one accessible platform
  • Support the visibility and growth of entities making positive change
  • Fund meaningful social projects through our affordable cell sales model
  • Build a global community united by shared values of sustainability and social responsibility
  • Democratize access to digital presence for entities of all sizes

Our Vision

We envision a world where every green entity, regardless of size or budget, has a voice and visibility in the global conversation about sustainability and social impact.

Our long-term vision is to collect as many entities as we can on this website to become the go-to destination for anyone seeking to discover, connect with, or support environmentally and socially responsible organizations worldwide. We see One Million Green Entities as more than a website – it's a movement that demonstrates how collective small actions can create substantial positive change.

By the time our grid is complete, we aim to have created a self-sustaining ecosystem where green entities thrive, social projects flourish, and positive impact multiplies across communities globally.

How We Select Entities

Our selection process ensures that every entity on our platform truly embodies green and socially responsible values and is not involved in any wars:

Core Criteria:

  • Peace Commitment: Entities must not be involved in any war or conflict-related activities
  • Positive Social Impact: Organizations must demonstrate clear positive impact on society or the environment
  • Mission Alignment: The entity's stated mission and vision must align with sustainability and social responsibility principles

Verification Process:

We thoroughly investigate each entity's mission, vision, and actual practices to confirm their environmental focus and social impact credentials. This includes reviewing their public communications, partnerships, and documented activities to ensure authenticity and alignment with our values.

Quality Standards:

We maintain high standards to preserve the integrity of our platform and ensure that every entity represents genuine commitment to positive change.

How to Be Part of Our Initiative

Joining One Million Green Entities is a simple and straightforward process designed to ensure quality while making participation accessible:

Step 1: Contact Us

Reach out to us through our website to express your interest in joining our platform. Let us know about your organization and your commitment to green and social impact initiatives.

Step 2: Choose Your Cells

Select the number of cells you want to purchase on our grid. Each cell costs just €2 and consists of 10,000 blocks, with each block containing 100 pixels where your logo and link will be displayed. You can choose one cell or multiple cells based on your needs and budget.

Step 3: Entity Verification

We conduct a thorough review of your organization to ensure it meets our green entity criteria. This includes examining your mission, vision, and actual practices to confirm your commitment to positive environmental and social impact.

Step 4: Payment Details

Once your entity passes our verification process, we'll contact you via email with all the payment details and instructions. We'll provide you with secure payment options and clear next steps.

Step 5: Reservation & Placement

After payment is completed, we reserve your chosen cells on the grid and upload your logo and website link. Your organization becomes part of our global showcase of green entities, visible to visitors worldwide.

Step 6: Go Live

Your entity is now live on our platform, contributing to both the global green community and funding our social impact projects. Welcome to the movement!

Our First Project: Supporting Immigrant Communities

Our inaugural social project focuses on creating a catering and kitchen initiative specifically designed to support immigrant communities. This project will be funded directly through the proceeds from our cell sales.

Project Goals:

  • Provide culinary training and employment opportunities for immigrants
  • Create a sustainable food service that serves the broader community
  • Build bridges between immigrant communities and local residents through food and culture
  • Establish a model for future social projects that can be replicated in other locations

Timeline: This project will launch once we reach our cell sales target, ensuring we have sufficient funding to make a meaningful impact.

Impact: Beyond providing immediate support to immigrant communities, this project demonstrates how digital innovation can drive real-world social change, creating a template for future initiatives funded through our platform.
