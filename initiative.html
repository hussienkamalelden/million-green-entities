Our Story

One Million Green Entities was born from a simple observation: amazing green organizations were scattered across the digital world, their incredible work often invisible to those who needed to find them.

We created this platform to solve that problem. Inspired by the concept of collective visibility, we're building a space where every green entity - from grassroots nonprofits to established eco-businesses - can stand together and be discovered.

Our Mission

To create the world's most comprehensive, accessible platform for green entities while funding meaningful social projects that support immigrant communities.

And connecting sustainable brands through engaging pixel space opportunities.

What Makes an Entity "Green"

We define green entities as organizations that:

  • Have positive environmental or social impact
  • Are not involved in any conflicts or wars
  • Demonstrate genuine commitment to making the world better
